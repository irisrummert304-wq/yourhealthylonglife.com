A Holistic Approach Rooted in Strategy

Your doctor says your results look okay and your lab values are 'within normal range.' But you sense something's not quite right - or simply not optimal. I help you bridge that gap from 'not sick' to truly thriving. My strategic approach optimizes your health proactively rather than reactively, helping you stay ahead of health issues before they become diagnoses.

As a certified Integrative Nutrition Health Coach with additional qualifications as a Personal Trainer and Nutritionist, I address all four dimensions of health: Mental (clarity, focus & strength), Physical (movement, nutrition & vitality), Spiritual (purpose, values & inner balance), and Emotional (intelligence & resilience).

My Mission: To empower busy professionals and parents to proactively take control of their health, moving from "just okay" to truly thriving - with energy, clarity, and vitality.

After a stressful period focused solely on career and family, several health crises - with myself but mostly within my close circle - as well as a sudden move to another continent, prompted me to pause and listen to my inner needs. I realized I didn't want to just feel 'okay' or 'not that tired' anymore. I wanted to feel truly energized, well-rested, and in top shape - for myself and my family.

Now, through Health Coaching, I want to help you do the same. You don't have to just function - you deserve to thrive. After years developing strategies in the pharmaceutical industry, I now help you create your own health strategy. Together, we'll proactively set your goals and steer your health journey before illness dictates the path.
